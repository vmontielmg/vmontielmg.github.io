How to: Mexican Horchata!

Ingredients

  • Milk. (1 1/2 cup)
  • Water. (1 liter)
  • Rice. (1 cup)
  • Cinnamon. (2 sticks)
  • Sweetened condensed milk. (1 can/ 12 oz.)
  • Vanilla extract. (1 tablespoon)
  • Evaporated milk. (1 can/ 12 oz.)

Non-edible items needed:

  • Blender.
  • Large bowl.
  • Strainer.
  • Large pitcher.
  • Large spoon or whisk.

Step 1:

+

Soak rice and cinnamon overnight(preferred) in a large bowl or for a couple of hours in warm water until rice is slightly soft.

Step 2:

Strain water out of soaked ingredients. Blend rice, cinnamon, evaporated and condensed milk until smooth and rice is completely ground. Strain blended mixture into large pitcher.

Step 3:

Add in the rest of ingredients into the pitcher and mix well.

Step 4:

Pour into a large glass of ice and enjoy!

Comments:

Some ingredients you can edit to your taste like I did with the sugar and the consensed milk. I like the sweetness of the sweetened condensed milk better than the sugar. You can add more things like the person I found this recipe from www.mylatinatable.com how she added almond shavings to soak and blend with the rice and cinnamon. You can add what you like or remove what you don't, it's all up to you and it will taste amazing!
